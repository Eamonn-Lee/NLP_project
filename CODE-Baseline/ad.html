Alfred Health is the main provider of health services to people living in the inner southeast suburbs of Melbourne and is also a major provider of specialist services to people across Victoria. The health service operates three outstanding facilities, The Alfred, Caulfield, and Sandringham.

An exciting opportunity exists for an enthusiastic and experienced Personal Assistant to provide executive support to the Clinical Service Director of Emergency and Acute Medicine, the Deputy Clinical Service Director and one other team member.

Your key duties and responsibilities will include

  • Support the Clinical Service Director, Deputy CSD and one other team member  
  • Diary management, meeting coordination, travel and conferences
  • Promote good customer relations, with patients, visitors and Hospital personnel
  • Customer service and clerical duties include answering telephones in a timely manner
  • Maintain patient confidentiality at all times and per Alfred Health policy
  • Provide information and reports as required   
  • Acts in a professional manner at all times
  • Attends appropriate meetings as required
  • Undertakes other duties as deemed appropriate by the Clinical Service Director

To succeed in this role you will have

  • Exemplary interpersonal skills, a professional, diplomatic, calm and accommodating demeanour
  • Excellent written skills - management of incoming/outgoing correspondence on behalf of the Clinical Service Director and other designated staff
  • Excellent oral skills - management of incoming/outgoing communications on behalf of the Clinical Service Director and other designated staff
  • High levels of attention to detail - problem solving, management of departmental interactions, handling sensitive and confidential documentation and retention of all relevant information for governance purposes
  • Exemplary  time  management  skills,- balancing  the  requirements  of  the  Clinical Service Director,  other designated staff and personal work flows
  • Strong computer/IT skills, Outlook database & diary management, Word, PowerPoint, Excel
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Ability to work independently and within a team environment
  • Motivated and enthusiastic with a positive attitude toward work and colleagues
  • Flexibility to attend to a variety of clerical duties

The Alfred Hospital offers many staff benefits, including generous salary packaging, a gym, library and staff parking facilities.
